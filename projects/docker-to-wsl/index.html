Docker to WSL

#  # 

I was recently assigned to develop a custom Linux distribution for WSL, and I opted to use Docker to create it. In the process of figuring out how to convert a Docker file into a functional WSL distro, I was struck by how few resources were available on this topic online. After I successfully navigated the steps required to make this conversion, I documented my findings here in the hopes that others may find them useful.
